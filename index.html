Offerings

We offer a retreat at which you can stay for as little as an hour, or as long as a month. Activities include guided yoga sessions, facials, body treatments, various spa experiences, massages, physical therapy, nutritional classes, and wellness classes. All activities are available for individuals, pairs, and groups. Feel free to visit us and walk the grounds, and ask us about our take-home spa kits!
